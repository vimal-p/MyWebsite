NIPM BUSINESS QUIZ

  1. Home
  2. Add Your Value

NIPM Trivandrum Chapter organizes Chapter Level Business Quiz every year for B-School Students. The contest is organized in connection with the NIPM National Conference. Team consists of Two Students per team from various Business Schools from Trivandrum and Kollam districts. The prize comprises of Cash Award for First and Second Prize winners and Certificate will also be issued to the First, Second and Third prize winners and to all the participants.

The First and Second Prize Winner Teams of Chapter Level Quiz will be entitled for Regional Business Quiz for which Cash prize Rs.20,000/- for First Winner Team, Rs.15,000/ for Second Prize Winner Team and Rs.10,000/- for Third Prize Winner Team.

The Winners of Regional Business Quiz will be eligible for NIPM National Business Quiz for which the Cash Prizes will be Rs.1,00,000/- for First Winning Team, Rs.75,000/- for Second Prize Team and Rs.50,000/- for Third Prize Team along with mementos to the winning teams.
