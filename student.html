NIPM Student Chapter in B-Schools

  1. Home
  2. Add Your Value

NIPM organizes Student Chapter in B-Schools. The students of the B-Shools are given the opportunity to organize conferences and seminars in their college. NIPM Trivandrum Chapter organizes interaction Industry programs for various B-School students. NIPM Trivandrum Chapter organizes 2 to 3 hour Interactive Sessions / Induction programs for various B-School Students in Kerala. The sessions are handled by eminent HR Professionals in various sectors of Industries to update the students about the current scenario in the Industrial sectors.

A mentor will also be nominated from the college to guide these students.

NIPM Trivandrum Chapter conducts lecture meetings/workshops/seminars in various management schools for the benefit of the management students.

NIPM Students Chapter was Inaugurated in Kerala Institute of Cooperative Management, Neyyar Dam, Asian School of Business, Kazhakootam and Rajadhani Business School, Attingal. The student members will be attached to the Trivandrum Chapter.

NIPM Students will be issued Membership Certificates. NIPM Student will be eligible for attending various training programmes conducted by the chapter at the reduced rate of fee.
